# GeoBeats Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 17, 2026

Personal Life Insights ("we," "us," "our") operates GeoBeats (the "App"), a
location-aware music streaming app that automatically switches curated
YouTube playlists as you travel between regions. This Privacy Policy
explains what information the App collects, how we use it, and the choices
you have.

By using GeoBeats, you agree to the collection and use of information as
described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Location Data
GeoBeats' core feature requires knowing your approximate or precise
geographic location, in both the foreground and background, so it can
detect when you cross into a new region and switch music automatically.
We collect:
- Precise GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) at intervals while the App
  is in use, and in the background if you grant background location
  permission.
- The region ("zone") your coordinates currently match, and the times you
  enter and leave each zone.

You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your device
settings, but doing so will disable GeoBeats' core automatic-switching
feature.

### b. Anonymous Account Identifier
GeoBeats does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any
personal registration details. On first launch, we silently generate a
random, anonymous device identifier (via Firebase Anonymous Authentication)
so your data — such as saved journeys — can be associated with your
install and kept private to you. This identifier is not linked to your
name, email address, or any other directly identifying information unless
you separately provide it to us (for example, by emailing us for support).

### c. Journey & Listening Data
If you use the optional "Record journey" feature, we store, under your
anonymous identifier:
- The sequence of regions you traveled through and the time you entered
  and left each one.
- The approximate GPS coordinates recorded at each region entry.
- The YouTube video IDs and titles of tracks that played during your
  journey.
- A computed distance for the journey.

This data is stored so you can view, replay, and share your own journey
history. It is not shared with other users.

### d. Advertising Data
GeoBeats is supported by Google AdMob, which may collect device
identifiers (such as advertising ID), IP address, coarse location, and ad
interaction data to serve and measure banner and interstitial ads. This
data is collected and processed by Google according to
[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Where
required by law (including in the EEA and UK), we request your consent
before serving personalized ads.

### e. Automatically Collected Technical Data
Like most apps, GeoBeats and its underlying services (Firebase, Google
Play Services / Apple frameworks) may automatically collect device model,
operating system version, app version, language settings, and
crash/diagnostic logs, used solely to keep the App stable and secure.

### f. Embedded YouTube Content
Music playback is delivered through an embedded YouTube player. When you
play a track, Google/YouTube may collect data about that interaction under
its own privacy policy and terms, independent of GeoBeats. See
[YouTube's Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms) and
[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the information above to:
- Detect which region you're in and automatically switch playlists.
- Save and let you replay or share your journey history.
- Operate, maintain, and improve the App.
- Serve and measure advertising that supports the free-to-use App.
- Diagnose crashes and technical issues.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

---

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA / UK Users)
Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we rely on:
- **Consent** — for precise/background location access and personalized
  advertising, which you can withdraw at any time.
- **Legitimate interests** — for core app functionality, security, and
  crash diagnostics, balanced against your rights.
- **Contract necessity** — where processing is required to provide a
  feature you've actively requested (e.g., saving a journey you chose to
  record).

---

## 4. How We Share Information
We share information only with:
- **Google Firebase** (Firestore database, Anonymous Authentication) —
  our backend infrastructure provider.
- **Google AdMob** — for serving and measuring advertising.
- **Google/YouTube** — for embedded video playback.

We do not sell data to third parties, and we do not share your journey
history with other users or the public unless you explicitly use the
App's share feature to export an image yourself.

---

## 5. Data Retention
- Journey data is retained until you delete it or request deletion (see
  Section 7).
- Your anonymous identifier and associated data persist for as long as you
  keep the App installed, unless you request deletion.

---

## 6. Your Rights

### GDPR / UK GDPR (EEA / UK residents)
You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your
data, and to object to or withdraw consent for processing. Contact us
using the details in Section 11 to exercise these rights.

### CCPA / CPRA (California residents)
You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request
deletion, correct inaccurate information, and opt out of the "sale" or
"sharing" of personal information (including for cross-context behavioral
advertising). We do not sell personal information for money. To exercise
these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy
GeoBeats is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum
age in your jurisdiction, e.g. 16 in parts of the EEA), and we do not
knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a
child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 8. International Data Transfers
Because we use Google Firebase and Google AdMob, your information may be
processed on servers located outside your country of residence, including
in the United States. Where required, such transfers are made using
appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 9. Security
We rely on Firebase's security infrastructure (including access-controlled
Firestore rules scoped to your anonymous identifier) to protect your data.
No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot
guarantee absolute security.
